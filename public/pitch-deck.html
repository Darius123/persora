The Problem
You asked ChatGPT.

They disagreed.

France
"Strong squad depth, experienced tournament pedigree, Mbappé at his peak — France are the clear favourites heading into 2026."

Brazil
"Brazil's five World Cup titles, deep squad, and 24 years of hunger since 2002 make them the most historically grounded pick for 2026."
You now have two authoritative answers. No way to verify which one was right. No permanent record. No accountability. The reasoning disappears when you close the tab.
AI intelligence is ephemeral by design. The debate happened. Nobody can prove it.
The Insight

accountability

The most valuable AI output isn't an answer — it's a traceable reasoning chain you can point to later.
Prediction market users don't just want a verdict — they want to know who called it and whether they'll own it.
Permanent storage transforms AI output from content into evidence. That's a different product category.
